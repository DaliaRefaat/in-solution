The Way to Help the Poor

One of the biggest reasons that you should donate is to remove poverty from the world. In 1981, the percentage of poverty was 44.3 and it has been reduced to 9.6% in 2015. It is all because of the donations and charities. With charity, we can help people fight diseases and they can send their children to schools. When people will not have to spend extra money on useless causes, they will be able to get out of poverty.

Everyone has a dream of making this world a better place. We all want the world where there is no poverty, diseases, and crimes. We have to understand that poverty is the biggest reason that leads to crimes. If we want to make this world a better place than donating to the nonprofit organizations and charities is the best solution. When people will get employed they will not become criminals and so our world will be filled with positivity.
